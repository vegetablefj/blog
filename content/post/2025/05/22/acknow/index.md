# Acknowledgement

*这是本科论文的致谢，写了大量非常抽象的内容*

*具体时间也不记得了，后来可能还改了好几回*

I extend my deepest gratitude to Assistant Professor Fan Yuwei for his scholarly guidance and steadfast supervision throughout this research endeavor. Special appreciation is owed to Assistant Professors Yu Chenglong and Zheng Zhiwei, Associate Professor Donovan William Ross Goodchild, and Professor Qiu Yu for their domain-specific insights, which profoundly enriched my academic journey.

The mentorship of Assistant Professor Huang Yi, Professor Li Si, Akito Futaki, and Yang Xiaokui transcended the mere transmission of theorems. My profound gratitude extends to Tsinghua University and Qiuzhen College for cultivating an intellectually vibrant environment that nourished the genesis of this work. Professor Shing-Tung Yau’s visionary leadership and unwavering support have been a beacon of inspiration. I further acknowledge the indispensable contributions of faculty and staff to administrative and logistical operations.

I owe a profound debt of gratitude to my family. To my parents, whose gift of life and sacrifice nurtured me through childhood, whose steadfast support anchored me during storms, and whose unwavering concern for my well-being reminded me that humanity transcends scholarship—this work stands as a testament to your invisible hands shaping every page.

To my colleagues Jin Baizhou, Liang Shiyixin, and Xie Baiting, I express sincere appreciation for their scholarly collaboration, and to Wang Zichang for his meticulous assistance in $\LaTeX$ typesetting.
